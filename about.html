Our Story

UbuntuCode was born in KwaZulu-Natal with a simple belief: Technology should serve people first. Founded while I’m in my second year studying Applied Mathematics and Mathematics at university, UbuntuCode blends rigorous problem-solving with a deep sense of community. We started by helping local businesses get online—fast, secure websites, clear branding, and practical SEO—and grew into a partner that handles hosting, maintenance, and compliance too. The name “Ubuntu” guides how we work: transparent, reliable, and collaborative. Every project is an opportunity to turn complex ideas into useful tools that help clients start, grow, and scale. We’re young, focused, and hungry to deliver measurable results—rooted in KZN, building for the world.
